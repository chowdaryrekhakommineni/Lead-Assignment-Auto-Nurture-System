## Project Overview
The Lead Assignment & Auto-Nurture System automates lead management within Salesforce. It intelligently routes incoming leads to appropriate sales reps based on predefined rules (region, product interest, priority). Automation using Flows, Apex triggers, validation rules, and integrations ensures timely follow-ups, accurate data, and improved lead conversion.

---

## Objectives
- Automate lead capture, validation, assignment, and follow-ups  
- Assign leads intelligently based on region, product, and priority  
- Reduce manual intervention and improve productivity  
- Maintain data accuracy and transparency  
- Generate real-time reports and dashboards  
- Enhance customer engagement

## System Architecture
- Modular architecture combining declarative (Flows, Process Builder) and programmatic (Apex, APIs) components  
- Leads processed through validation, duplicate checks, and assignment rules  
- Notifications and auto-nurture workflows trigger follow-ups  
- Profiles, Roles, and Sharing Rules enforce access control  

---

## Data Model
- **Lead:** Captures potential customer info  
- **Account & Contact:** Converted from qualified leads  
- **Opportunity:** Tracks potential sales deals  
- **Custom Objects:** Lead assignment, follow-up tracking, approvals  
- Relationships: Lookup & Master-Detail to maintain integrity
